Discussion: should CS2 Trade Protected have a very limited retroactive review for earlier verified cases?


Valve introduced Trade Protected for CS2 items in July 2025, which allows trades to be reversed within 7 days.

It’s a good system and clearly addresses account compromise and scam trades.

The discussion I’m curious about is whether a very limited retroactive review could ever make sense for rare verified cases where:
• the compromise was confirmed
• the receiving account was banned
• the items never entered the market

The point wouldn’t be to reopen every old trade, but to discuss whether very narrow cases like this could theoretically be reviewed without affecting the market economy.
